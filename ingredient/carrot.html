Carrot

1. Storage

Fridge temperature: not necessary unless need longer storage time

Room temperature: 4 to 5 days for carrots, 2 days for baby carrots

2. Characteristics

  • Provides: Carrots are a particularly good source of beta carotene, fiber, vitamin K1, potassium, and antioxidants
  • Shouldn’t go with: orange - eating carrots and oranges together is not advised as this combination might lead to excess bile reflux and may even cause heartburn.

3. Recommended dish: Carrot Noodle Pad Thai

a. Ingredient

  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 3 tablespoons very hot water
  • ¼ cup palm sugar, grated if it is in a block
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce, gluten-free if needed
  • 4 medium carrots
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil, divided
  • 1 medium onion, sliced thin
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 10 prawns
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 2 green onions,sliced
  • 1 handful cilantro, stems torn off
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Sriracha, chopped chilis, extra green onions, cilantro, whole peanuts, or almonds, to serve
