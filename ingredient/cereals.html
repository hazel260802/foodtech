Cereals

1. Storage

Fridge temperature: cooked cereals such as oatmeal will only keep for 4 to 5 days in the fridge once prepared

Room temperature: When it's unopened, breakfast cereal lasts 6 to 8 months past the date on the box. If opened, your favorite cereal will typically stay fresh for between 4 and 6 months

2. Characteristics

  • Provides: source of carbohydrate and good source of B group vitamins
  • Shouldn’t go with: should avoid cereals containing these 6 ingredients of sugar, ultra-refined grains, mycotoxins, synthetics vitamins and minerals, glyphosate and GMOs
