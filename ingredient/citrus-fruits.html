Citrus fruits

1. Storage

Fridge temperature: about a week and a half

Room temperature: three or four days on the counter

2. Characteristics

  • Provides: contain carbohydrate, fiber, vitamin C, potassium, folate, calcium, thiamin, niacin, vitamin B6, phosphorus, magnesium, copper, riboflavin, pantothenic acid and a variety of phytochemicals
  • Shouldn’t go with: sweet fruits, milk

3. Recommended dish: Sesame Orange Vinaigrette

a. Ingredient

  • 1 mandarin orange, juiced (about ¼ cup)
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 3 tablespoons EACH: toasted sesame oil and avocado oil, or another light flavored oil
  • 2 tablespoons EACH: rice wine vinegar, honey, and soy sauce , gluten-free, if needed
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • ½ teaspoon EACH: finely minced ginger and garlic

b.Instructions

  • Place all the ingredients in a 2 cup glass jar and give it a good shake. It's ready to go when it's creamy.
  • Note: if your tahini is very thick, mix it with the orange juice first then add the rest of the ingredients and shake.
