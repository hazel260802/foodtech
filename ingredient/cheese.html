Cheese

1. Storage

Fridge temperature:

  • Hard cheese: 4 months in the fridge and 8 months in the freezer, if unopened.
  • Medium cheese: can be kept in a fridge for 2 to 3 weeks after they have been opened. In the freezer, this type of cheese can last for 2 months if the packet is still intact.
  • Soft cheese: Once opened, the cheese must be munched within a fortnight

Room temperature: not recommended

2. Characteristics

  • Provides: calories, sodium, calcium and fat
  • Shouldn’t go with: Citrus or high-acid fruits, spicy things,vegetal of vegetables (like broccoli, carrots, green beans, celery, and cauliflower)

3. Recommended dish: Broccoli Cheese Soup

a. Ingredient

  • 2 heads broccoli
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 medium onions chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed with the side of your knife
  • 1 medium russet potato, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon EACH: sea salt and pepper
  • 8 ounces extra-sharp cheddar cheese, grated

b.Instructions

  • Chop the broccoli stems and set them aside. Chop the broccoli florets into bite-sized pieces.
  • Melt the butter in a large soup pot over medium-high heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook until the onions are translucent, about 3 minutes.
  • Add the broccoli stems, potato, chicken stock, salt, and pepper to the pot and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes.
  • Add HALF the broccoli florets and simmer the soup for another 5 minutes, or until the potato and broccoli stems are soft.
  • Blend the soup using an immersion blender or carefully working in batches with a regular blender.
  • Add the remaining broccoli and the cheddar cheese to the pot and stir well. Let the broccoli cook for 3-4 minutes to soften the broccoli.
  • 8 ounces extra-sharp cheddar cheese
