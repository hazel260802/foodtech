Tomato

1. Storage

Fridge temperature: 2 weeks in the fridge as long as it’s already ripened.

Room temperature: 1 week on the counter

2. Characteristics

  • Provides: Tomatoes are the major dietary source of the antioxidant lycopene , which has been linked to many health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and cancer. They are also a great source of vitamin C, potassium, folate and vitamin K.
  • Shouldn’t go with: cucumber ( minor effect on digestive system)

3. Recommended dish: Cucumber Tomato Salad

a. Ingredient

  • 1 lb cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 1 English cucumber, quartered lengthwise then sliced
  • ¼ cup each: minced red onion, parsley, and cilantro(see notes)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, see notes
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • ½ teaspoon each: salt and pepper
  • Optional: ¼ cup feta cheese, omit for a dairy-free salad

b.Instructions

  • Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and cilantro to a large bowl.
  • Whisk the olive oil with the minced garlic, salt, and pepper and pour over the salad and toss to coat.
  • If you are using the feta cheese, crumble it over top of the salad and toss to mix it in.
